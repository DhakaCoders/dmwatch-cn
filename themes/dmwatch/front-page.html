DM WATCH

Disaster Management Watch (DM.WATCH) was established in 2013 as a Research, Training and Consultancy firm with a multi-disciplinary group of young professionals. It was initiated by a group of Researchers, Planners, Disaster Managers, Development Workers, Environmental Management Experts, Sociologists, Economists and Engineers.We are working in major thematic sections to promote livable community and ensure sustainability.

DM.WATCH has a history of working with renowned organizations such as United Nations Development Program, Urban Development Directorate, WaterAid, Local Government Engineering Department,Save the Children, SNV, PLAN International, Concern Worldwide etc. It has significant contribution in upholding the national policy through innovative research ideas.
